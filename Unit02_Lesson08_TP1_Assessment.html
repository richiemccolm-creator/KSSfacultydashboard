Melodrama Performances
1
Each group performs. Audience — boo the villain, cheer the hero, gasp at the twist. Your reaction is part of the performance.
2
Performers — commit to every moment. Every movement is deliberate. Every pause is intentional. Every reaction is exaggerated.
3
After each performance: one minute of class feedback using S.P.E.C.S. and Drama vocabulary — name one convention used and one element that was strongest.
PERFORMERS — ONE FINAL REMINDER
This is a performance — not a rehearsal. Commit to it completely. If something goes wrong — stay in character, stay in the world, keep going.
After all groups have performed, you will complete your written evaluation. Make notes in your head as you watch — you will need specific examples to write about.
