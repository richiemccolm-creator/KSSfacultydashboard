What is a Freeze Frame?

Definition

A stage picture held completely still — a frozen image that represents a moment, emotion or idea.

  • Everyone in the group freezes at exactly the same moment
  • Your body, face and position all communicate meaning
  • The audience must understand the story without words
  • Think about levels, proxemics and body language in every freeze

Unit 01 — All Vocabulary

All 7 words from this unit

These are the words to use in your evaluation and written reflection today.

BL
Body Language
Communicating non-verbally through the position and movement of the body.
EC
Eye Contact
Using direct gaze to show relationships, power or emotion between performers.
PS
Posture
How you stand and carry your body to represent character or status.
UL
Use of Levels
Using different heights on stage to show status and create visual variety.
PX
Proxemics
The use of space and distance between performers to communicate relationships and tension.
ST
Status
How much power a character has — shown through levels, eye contact, proximity and posture.
MA
Mood & Atmosphere
Mood is the tone of the drama. Atmosphere is the feeling the audience gets from watching.
