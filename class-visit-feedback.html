What this looks like

3
Learning Intention
QI 2.3 — Quality of Teaching
What this looks like
  • Makes purpose of learning clear
  • Expressed as 'To know, to understand or be able to'
  • Separated from learning context
  • Focused on skills and knowledge, not tasks
  • Shared verbally and visually in pupil friendly language
  • Demonstrate an awareness of prior learning
  • Define the learning for a lesson or series of lessons
  • Revisited during the learning process

Success Criteria
QI 2.3 — Quality of Teaching
What this looks like
  • Pupils actively involved in co-creation of success criteria
  • Expressed as 'I can' statements
  • Makes clear what success looks like in a lesson or series of lessons
  • Used to support feedback, self and peer assessment

Differentiation
QI 2.3 — Quality of Teaching; Learning & Engagement
What this looks like
  • Clear presentation, including explanations and modelling of content
  • Teacher support provided throughout lesson
  • Evidence of collaborative learning to allow young people to support each other
  • Scaffolds provided for learners
  • Evidence of pupil choice within activities
  • Additional challenge provided (e.g. increased difficulty or extension work)
