CREATE· Warm-up activity 28–45 min
28–45

Name & Gesture Circle
1
Stand in a circle as a class
2
Say your name and do a gesture that represents you
3
The whole group copies your name and gesture together
4
Move clockwise until everyone has had a turn
5
Final round — can you remember everyone's name and gesture?
Focus on: eye contact · clear gesture · listening · energy — commit to the gesture!
DEBRIEF
