Score each Tracking Period across Art & Design units — Progress (1–4 scale) plus Effort, Behaviour, and Home Learning. Six TPs per year align to unit completion (order varies by teacher). Progress Analysis shows gaps across the year. Export clean CSV for dashboard reporting.
What is tracked at each TP?
Progress
1–4 scale — practical work, design solutions, evaluation against brief. Make, Write, Say, Do modes.
Effort
Engagement and application in practical and written work.
Behaviour
Classroom conduct and collaboration.
Home Learning
Completion and quality of homework and sketchbook work.
