Main Activity  ·  Create

Trust Exercises

Work in groups of 4–5. Your teacher will assign an exercise or you may choose one.

1
Mirror Work  — face your partner and copy their movements exactly. Take turns to lead. No touching.
2
Blind Walk  — one person closes their eyes. Their partner guides them safely around the space using voice only.
3
Status Walk  — walk around the space. Use your levels, posture and eye contact to communicate a status: high, medium or low.

Vocabulary Focus  ·  New Today

Three key words for this unit

Use these in your evaluation and written reflection.

PX
Proxemics
The use of space and distance between performers to communicate relationships and power.
e.g. Standing very close can suggest threat or intimacy
ST
Status
How much power and importance a character has — communicated through levels, eye contact, proximity and posture.
e.g. A character who stands tall and holds eye contact reads as high status
MA
Mood & Atmosphere
Mood is the tone or feeling of the drama. Atmosphere is the feeling the audience gets from watching it.
e.g. Slow, low-level movement creates a tense atmosphere
