Privacy Policy

Expressive Arts Faculty · Knightswood Secondary School · Last updated March 2025

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the Curriculum Design and Report Builder application.

Data processors

We use Supabase as our cloud provider for storing data. Our agreement with Supabase includes a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) compliant with UK GDPR. Data is stored in the UK/EU region where you deploy your project.

Data we collect

We collect and store:

  • Account data: Email address, name (if provided via sign-in)
  • Pupil data: Names, year groups, attainment scores, report comments, and related educational information that you enter into the trackers and report builders
  • Faculty data: Academic calendar events, moderation notes, and departmental improvement plan content

Legal basis

We process pupil data under legitimate interest for the purpose of delivering education and supporting teaching, assessment, and reporting at Knightswood Secondary School. This is necessary for the school to fulfil its educational duties.

Data storage and security

Data is stored in a secure cloud database (Supabase) with encryption at rest. Access is restricted to authenticated users only. Each user can only access their own data. All connections use HTTPS.

Data retention

Pupil data is retained for the duration of the school year plus one year, unless you request earlier deletion. You may export or delete your data at any time (see below).

Your rights under GDPR

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (export a copy)
  • Rectification of inaccurate data
  • Erasure (delete your account and all associated data)
  • Data portability (receive your data in a machine-readable format)

Your data rights

If you are signed in, you can exercise your rights below.

Pupil and parent rights

Pupil data (names, progress, effort, behaviour, home learning) is processed for educational purposes. Pupils and parents/carers have the right to access, rectification, and erasure of pupil data. To exercise these rights, please contact the school office or Data Protection Officer. Requests will be processed in line with our statutory duties and within 30 days.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (session management). No analytics or tracking cookies are used.

Contact

For questions about this policy, data subject access requests, or to contact the Data Protection Officer, please contact the school office at Knightswood Secondary School.
