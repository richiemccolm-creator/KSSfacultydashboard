THE FRAMEWORK· Learn this — you will use it every lesson this unit 8–20 min
S.P.E.C.S.
Every mime you perform must follow these five principles
S
SIMPLE
One clear action at a time. The audience must read you in one glance.
P
PRECISE
Every movement is deliberate. No fussing — commit to the exact shape.
E
EXAGGERATED
Bigger than real life. Make every gesture count from a distance.
C
CLEAR
No ambiguity. If the audience can't tell what you're doing, the mime has failed.
S
SLOW
Slowing down makes movement readable. Rushing destroys clarity.
